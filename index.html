What we do
We help organizations unlock the power of AI through custom inference,
retrieval-augmented generation, and intelligent data integration.




AI-Powered Solutions

We design and deploy production AI systems using large language models, building custom inference pipelines tailored to your business needs. We turn unstructured data into actionable intelligence and help organizations identify high-impact AI use cases that deliver real value.

RAG & Knowledge Systems

We build retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) pipelines over your proprietary data, handling document ingestion, chunking, embedding, and vector search. Our systems ground AI responses in your organization's knowledge base, ensuring accuracy, relevance, and reduced hallucination.

Data Integration & AI Infrastructure

We connect AI systems to enterprise data sources including databases, APIs, and documents, building robust data pipelines that feed inference systems. We architect scalable AI infrastructure for production workloads and monitor and optimize AI system performance and costs.
