What we do

AI-Powered Solutions

We design and deploy production AI systems that solve real business problems. Whether it's automating document review, building intelligent chatbots for customer support, or creating internal tools that let your team query data in plain English — we build AI that your people actually use.
  • Custom AI applications using Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, and xAI models
  • Intelligent chatbots and conversational AI for customers and internal teams
  • AI strategy workshops to identify your highest-ROI use cases
  • Proof-of-concept to production in weeks, not months

RAG & Knowledge Systems

Your organization sits on years of valuable documents, policies, reports, and institutional knowledge. We build systems that make all of it instantly searchable and queryable through natural language — so your team gets accurate, sourced answers instead of generic AI responses.
  • Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) over your proprietary documents
  • Document ingestion pipelines: PDFs, emails, wikis, Slack, SharePoint
  • Answers grounded in your data with source citations
  • Reduced hallucination through retrieval-first architecture

Data Integration & AI Infrastructure

AI is only as good as the data behind it. We connect your AI systems to the data sources that matter — databases, APIs, CRMs, ERPs, and document stores — and build the infrastructure to keep it all running reliably at scale.
  • Enterprise data connectors for Salesforce, SAP, internal databases, and more
  • Scalable vector search and embedding infrastructure
  • Production deployment on AWS, Azure, or your private cloud
  • Cost monitoring and optimization for inference workloads
