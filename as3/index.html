What We Do
DevOps & Config Management
We provide expert consulting in DevOps and configuration management. We are well versed in many orchestration tools, including Puppet, Chef and Ansible. We've converted our passion for writing code by treating infrastructure as code and provide elegant solutions to our clients, helping reduce cost.
Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is one of the the most complex changes to computing. The importance of cloud computing and it's uniqueness distinguish the scalability of an application. We help clients establish a "cloud" environment that is secure, stable, and cost effective.
Monitoring

We help clients provide near 100% uptime of their solutions to end-users. We do this by integrating industry standard and custom monitoring solutions to help diagnose problems before and while they exist. Catering to the needs of our clients, we use standard industry tools (riemann, nagios, New Relic, Boundary, etc) to provide monitoring of your entire infrastructure.
